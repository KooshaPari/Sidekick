# Sidekick Brand Identity

## Overview
**Sidekick** — Your companion framework for distributed, resilient task orchestration.

**Tagline:** Reliability at scale.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Accent | #F97316 | Primary interactive elements, CTAs, highlights |
| Neutral | #6B7280 | Body text, secondary UI, backgrounds |

## Typography

- **Monospace** (code, technical UI): IBM Plex Mono, Roboto Mono, or system monospace
- **Body** (prose, labels): Inter, Helvetica Neue, or system sans-serif

## Visual Identity

**Logo Placeholder:** `assets/logo-placeholder.svg` (256x256 SVG, uppercase "S" in warm orange)

**Symbol Rationale:** The letter "S" evokes *speed* and *sidekick* role—a framework that moves alongside your primary systems. The warm orange (#F97316) conveys energy, approachability, and reliability without being corporate.

## Brand Voice

Sidekick speaks with a clear, practical tone. We emphasize resilience and distributed computing principles without unnecessary jargon. Our copy is direct: explaining *what* the system does and *why* it matters for operator confidence and system reliability. We assume the audience understands infrastructure and prefers straightforward explanations over marketing flourish.
